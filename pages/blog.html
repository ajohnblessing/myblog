Falling in love
Falling in love is the development of strong feelings of attachment and love,
usually towards another person. The termis metaphorical, emphasing
that the process, like the physical act of falling, is sudden, uncontrollable
and leaves the lover in a vulnerable state, similar to "fall ill".
Falling in love is easily one of the best feelings ever. That is why there
are so many articles, songd, movies and reality shows about love amd relationship
Its an essential part of our lives which sometimes controls our mood; either we are
happy, contented or heartbroken. These feelings tend to affect us more than we even
realise. Being in a healthy relationship helps to boost our self confidence which in
turn boost other areas of our life. The feeling of knowing someone has your back and
would always stand by you no matter what is one of the major reasons people venture
into relationships because we feel safe and secured with our partners.

Some Signs which shows you are in love with a person

  • You can't stop staring at them
  • You feel unusually optimistic around them
  • You always think about them
  • They can do no wrong
  • Time flies when you are together
  • You dont mind when they do some unattractive
  • You want to touch and kiss them (intimacy).

Intimacy
Intimacy in a relationship is a feeling of
being close, and emotionally connected and
suppoted. It means being able to share a whole
range of thoughts, feelings and experiences that
we have as human beings. It involves being open
and talking through your thoughts and emotions,
letting your guards down (being vulnerable)and
showing someone else how you feel and what hopes
and dreams you have. Intimacy is build overtime,
and requireseffort and patience from both partners
to create and maintain. Discovering intimacy with
someone you love can be one most rewarding aspect of a
relatonship. Apart from sexual and emotional intimacy,
you can also be intimate recreationally, intellectually,
financially, creatively and at times of crisis. Intimacy
is achieved when we become close to someone else and are
reassured and accepted for who we are.
For many couples, 'making love' requires a sense of imtimacy
and emotional closeness. An intimate sexual relationship involves
trust and being vulnerable with one another. Closeness during sex
is also linke to other forms of intimacy including emotional and
spiritual intimacy. Intercourse is only a part of sexual intimacy
which involves foreplay and other forms of physical intimacy.
Explore different ways to share love and affectuin without sex
and remember that sex includes, many different forms of physical
contact. Often, the more a couple is intimate with each other in
ways other than sex, the more fulfilling their sex life becomes.

5 Signs your partner is loosing interest in you

  • Your partner feels more like a roommate

  • You haven't had sex in a while

    In a similar vein, if you are usec to falling into bed pratically every night,
    it might seem like a red flag if your partner is suddenly less interested in sex.
    It is very important to keep it in mind that many spouses have sex less often as their
    relationshipgoes on and probably once the honeymoon phase comes to an end.

  • Your conversations feels forced

    There are number of reasons why a conversation might feel strained. especially
    if you recently argued, went through a tough time together or expressed deep feeling
    Your partner may be acting weird because they mifht have recently shared something
    that made them feel vulnerable with you and they aren't sure how to act around you
    at the moment. If that's the case, it's only natural for things to feel dry afterwards
    But if your text exchange and calls always feel dry afterwards, that would definitely be
    "Questionable".

  • They Don't Call or Text As Much

    While there vare lots of reasons why a partner might drop their texting game for a short
    period of time such as a busy schedule, it is often a sign that they are loosing interest
    and therefore aren't willing to make talking to you a priority.

  • They won't define the relationship

    Not everyone often feel nthe need to label a relationship, a lack of willingness or commitment
    to do so can certainly indicates that a person has lost inerest. So if you are a few months into
    the relationship and still don't know who you "are" to each other, consider it a red flag.

How to win your partner's love and attention back

  • Working to rebuild intimacy

    By asking and queries like "what were the things we did in the beginning of our relationship
    that made us feel loved and appreciated?" Prioritise those fun and sweet things and see if it
    helps bring you back together again.

  • Talk about your sex life

    It is often tough to talk about sex, try to find some time to discuss with your partner
    about the changes you noticed and the amount of sex you'd like to have, and then see if
    there is a way to make both of you happy.

  • Focus on your own life and success

    Guys like girls who have focus whether its in their studies, careers, sport or any other actitvity.
    Show him that you are a woman with dreams, ambition and passion. Focus on your own life and vigorously
    pursue whatever you are doing at the moment. Men are naturally attracted to successful women: if you
    manage to become one, there is little more you will hsve to do to make him want you morer and more.

  • Be honest

    Honesty is one simple word, but probably the most important aspect of a relationship, and certainly an
    essential part of regaining love that may have lapsed. I'm sure you were honest at some points in your
    bonding, when nothing seemed like a taboo, you could tell your partner everything there was to know about
    yourself, try to work on your honesty and see your partner back in your arms.

  • Trust one another

    Following from the last step and working hand in hand with it, trust is the key to a lasting bond of love. If you
    trust your partner, then love is not apparent if we allow untrusting thoughts consume us. Regaining love without
    first regaining trust will be an uphill struggle and so start to work through you insecure feelings as soon as possible.
    Ask yourself why you have lost the faith you once had in your partner, and try to regain that and make things right again.
